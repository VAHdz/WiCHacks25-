Minimum Travel Requirements Per Day

💧 Water

Minimum: 2-3 liters per person

🍲 Food

Three meals per day (Budget or mid-range restaurants)

🏨 Accommodation

Budget options: Hostels, budget hotels, Airbnb

🚍 Local Transportation

Public metro, buses, taxis, rental bikes/scooters

📶 Internet & SIM

Buy a local SIM card or get an international roaming plan

💰 Emergency Cash

Carry at least $50-$100 in local currency for emergencies
